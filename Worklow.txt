The webcam feed is processed frame-by-frame.
Haar Cascade models detect the face and eyes.
Based on the number of detected eyes:
0 eyes → considered Sleeping
1 eye → considered Drowsy
2+ eyes → considered Active
State counters track how long a condition persists to prevent false triggers.
Alarms:
  Sleeping: continuous beep until eyes reopen.
  Drowsy: four short beeps.
